# Why this works (Corporate UI/UX logic)

- **Blue** → Trust, education, stability
- **Maroon** → Academic tradition, authority
- **Purple** → Knowledge, higher learning
- **Orange** → Energy, announcements
- **Green** → Success actions (login, payment)

This palette keeps the site **corporate, clean, and academic**.
